About us

There is an old cliche "a woman's place is in the home." Woman have broken the mould and proved their competence in different fields disapproving the said cliche. We have devised online tests for selection in certain career paths which are still considered male dominated. Both, boys and girls can take the tests. Results will be surprising!

User Guide

  • Visit the Challenging Careers website and create an account on it for free.
  • Once you create an account you have option to appear for any of the given test on our website.
  • After getting logged in, hover over the Exams tab to see the Services available.
  • Choose the service you want to apply for.
  • Relevant question paper appears on the screen.
  • Attempt the question paper in the given time.
  • After completing the question paper click the submit button.
  • The result appears on a New Page.
